Success Stories

Selimedu (State of Puducherry)

A dispensary was created in 2003 in this village located 15 km southwest of Puducherry, with the financial support of the European Union as part of a 5-year partnership.

The site consists of two buildings, now used by Satya School, a socio-educational structure welcoming children with special needs daily.

About fifty children are supervised by specialised educators. The Mahatma Gandhi Medical College offers a weekly medical consultation, ensuring solidarity with the rural population while training its interns.

Mevalur Dispensary (Tamil Nadu)

A dispensary was established in 2000 to provide medical care in this village located 40 km west of Chennai.

Since 2016, a partnership with The Candles association has supported daily social action: school support, library, water, sanitation in the village, and training projects (sewing, etc.).

The Candles are supported by the Praise Foundation. The government of Puducherry finances a weekly medical consultation with Dr. ROBIN for all the villagers.
