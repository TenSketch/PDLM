Selimedu – A Success Story

What began in 2003 as a small dispensary with EU support has today transformed into Satya School, a socio-educational space caring for over 50 children with special needs in Selimedu village.

Our partner: Sharing in the world, Puducherry

The Indian association Partage dans le monde Puducherry was created in 1995. Dr. NALLAM, president since its creation, was succeeded by Mrs. Chitra SHAH in 2013. The association is authorized to receive funds from abroad (FCRA: Foreign Contribution Regulation Act).
